The Spectrum Codes



The Spectrum Codes is an advocacy group to prepare neurodiverse individuals for careers in technology.

Purpose:

  • To explore programming,
  • Help you get work ready,
  • To show that programming is an accessible career,
  • That companies are actively recruiting autistic people,
  • Helping with social and interview skills,
  • Advocacy group

How do we do that?

  • Interview prep
  • Social skills development
  • Mentor support
  • Community engagement
  • Educating larger tech community on the abilities and benefits of neurodiverse individuals in the workplace
